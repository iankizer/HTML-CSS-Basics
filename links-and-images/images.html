Images

This page covers common image formats, but you may also be looking for links and useful extras.

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos.

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations.

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons.

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. Use them wherever you can. They are good for pretty much all the same use cases as PNGs, and you should use them whenever you can. SVG is a vector-based graphics format, meaning it can scale up or down to any dimension without loss of quality.
